    My name is Jeffrey Goldman

    I am 26 years old and I currently live in Shimokitazawa in Tokyo.

    For the past 4 years, I have been living and working in Miyazaki Prefecture as an ALT on the JET Program. I taught English to Middle School, Elementary School, and Kindergarten age students in the small rural town of Kijo. It was a beautiful place filled with some of the most incredible, kind, and fun people I've ever met, but I've also always wanted to live in a big city and finally made the move to Tokyo at the beginning of August, 2015.
